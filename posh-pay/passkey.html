Passkey iframe test

Step 1: Get an account on webauthn.tornadored.com

  1. Go to webauthn.tornadored.com, pick a username and click register.
  2. On iOS 16, you will be asked to save a passkey.
  3. See that it works, and tap logout.

Step 2: Log in to your account via a cross-origin iframe

  1. You are now on a different domain (this one), which embeds webauthn.tornadored.com in an iframe, which sets allow="publickey-credentials-get"
  2. Enter the same username you chose earlier, and tap "login"
  3. You should be able to use your passkey cross-origin!
